THE MODERN ART GALLERY

The Modern Art Gallery features various modern, postmodern and contemporary installations, private gallery openings and fine museums to feed your need of the Modern. Discover intriguing artworks shows the departure from traditional styles, in search of new forms and expressions emerging in the late 19th and early 20th century. Take a journey through experimentation, abstraction, and mind-bending creative explorations.

EXHIBITIONS

EXPLORE OUR PERMANENT COLLECTIONS

The Collection of Art after 1945 comprises over 10,000 artworks, dating from the Second World War to today. The Collection of Modern and Contemporary Art is joined with the Collection of Architecture, which has been taking care of more than 20,000 items.

MODERN PAINTING

The section dedicated to painting from 1907 to 1939 presents 67 seminal works of modern art representing the generation of the founders of modern art, the Expressionists, the Cubists from the period before the First World War, and the interwar avant-garde artists from around the year 1900.

MODERNIST PHOTOGRAPHY

One of the world's greatest private collections of photography, drawn from the classic modernist period of the 1920s-50s, with over 70 artists and nearly 150 rare vintage prints on show. An incredible group of Man Ray portraits are exhibited together for the first time

AVANT-GARDE CINEMA

Addressing highly influential films and directors related to international surrealism during the second half of the twentieth century, it expands the purview of both surrealism and film studies by situating surrealism as a major force in postwar cinema.

ACHITECTURE COLLECTION

The collection focuses mostly on post-1945 architecture. The collection is divided into a section of plans, another for drawings, photography and negatives, models, and an independent section for prints with architectonic themes.
